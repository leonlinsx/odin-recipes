Awesome lasagna

Description

Here's how to make a classic lasagna with beef, onion, garlic, and more!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24-ounce jar of marinara sauce
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (about a small handful)
  • 9x13-inch baking dish
  • Uncooked lasagna noodles (9-12, depending on dish size)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and cook until the onion is soft.
  3. Stir in the marinara sauce and let it simmer while you prepare the other ingredients.
  4. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese with the egg and chopped fresh parsley.
  5. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of the baking dish.
  6. Add a layer of uncooked lasagna noodles over the meat sauce.
  7. Spread a layer of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  8. Top with a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese and a sprinkling of grated Parmesan cheese.
  9. Repeat the layers two more times, ending with a final layer of meat sauce and a topping of shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese.
  10. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  11. Remove the foil and continue baking for another 25 minutes, or until the lasagna is bubbly and golden on top.
  12. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before cutting and serving.

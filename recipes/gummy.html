gum gum fruit

Description

Gomu gomu

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon unflavored gelatin
  • 2 tablespoons cold water
  • 1/2 cup fruit juice (apple, orange, or berry)
  • 1 tablespoon honey or sugar
  • Food coloring (optional)
  • Gummy bear molds
  • Dropper or pipette

Steps

  1. Bloom the gelatin by sprinkling it over two tablespoons of cold water in a small bowl. Let it sit for 5 minutes.
  2. In a small saucepan, heat the fruit juice and honey (or sugar) over medium heat until dissolved, stirring gently.
  3. Add the bloomed gelatin to the hot juice mixture and stir until completely melted.
  4. Optionally, add a few drops of food coloring to achieve the desired color for your gummy bears.
  5. Use a dropper or pipette to carefully fill gummy bear molds with the mixture.
  6. Refrigerate the filled molds for at least one hour, or until the gummy bears are set.
  7. Once set, remove the gummy bears from the molds by gently pushing from the bottom.
  8. Store the gummy bears in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to a week.
